YSL Black Opium neon | Dissolving in particles

In this small personal project, I decided to experiment with particle-dissolving simulations and tie them to the concept of beauty.

I picked the YSL Black Opium Neon bottle, an iconic and groundbreaking design that has been making waves since the '70s.

A mix of fashion and fragrance that grabs attention and delights.

Hope you 'll enjoy it :)

Here I want to show a little bit of process, I used pyrosourcespread SOP to create an attributes to use them as particle emitter and dissolving topology, also I created custom velocity fields to direct particles, then I successfuly exported all attributes with help of geometry nodes in Blender.

Type: Full CGI
Client: Personal project
Product: Yves Saint Laurent Black Opium
3D Work: Full Process
Softwares: Houdini (particles), Blender &Cycles, Adobe After Effects.

Thanks for watching! :)
