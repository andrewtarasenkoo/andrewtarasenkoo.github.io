Gucci Flora Personal project

In this project, I wanted to play with the blend of nature and elegance through 3D animation.

I picked the Gucci Flora bottle —a classic design known for its vibrant, feminine touch. The animation shows flowers gently growing around the bottle, reflecting the mix of luxury and natural beauty.

Hope you enjoy it as much as I enjoyed creating it :)

Type: Full CGI
Client: Personal project
Product: Gucci Flora - Gorgeous Gardenia
3D Work: Full Process
Softwares: Houdini, Blender &Cycles, Adobe After Effects.

Thanks for watching! :)
