Viral VFX ad

Recently, I had a chance to work with "DDD "Creative Communications Agency. My goal was to create a viral VFX animation for the leading packaging company "RIVNE FLEX "to increase follower engagement.

I was given several videos of the city center, and we agreed on the idea of animation and shape, and the design of the candy.

Short process:
‍
1. Camera tracking.
2. Modeling collisions.
3. Modeling Candy 's

4. Simulations.
‍
Each candy has a proxy cage topology in order to optimize simulations.
Also I created vertex map that will define softness of the candy 's.

Thanks for watching!
